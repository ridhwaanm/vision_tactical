Paramedics from ER24 will respond to any emergencies in Houghton and surrounding suburbs with certain Vision Tactical Response Units. ER24 owns its fleet of more than 280 Rapid Response Vehicles and Ambulances, and operates a national 24-hour Emergency Contact Centre.

"We are taking our service to the next level making our tactical response unique," said Yaseen Theba, Director of Vision Tactical. "We partnered with experts in our field that offer quality medical emergency service solutions to best suit our clients' needs," he added.

For enquiries, contact Vision Tactical at 082 494 2824.
