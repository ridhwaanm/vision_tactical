As South Africa held the G20 presidency in 2025, Vision Tactical was honoured to play a supporting role in ensuring the safety of visiting delegations and the broader community.

## Our Contribution

During the G20-related events held in Johannesburg, we deployed additional resources to support SAPS and other law enforcement agencies. This included:

- **Enhanced Patrols**: Increased armed response patrols in areas hosting G20 events
- **Drone Surveillance**: Aerial monitoring of event venues and surrounding areas
- **Control Room Coordination**: Dedicated controllers liaising with SAPS command
- **VIP Protection Support**: Assistance with perimeter security for certain venues

## Community Safety First

While the G20 brought international attention to South Africa, our primary focus remained on protecting our local communities. The additional resources deployed during this period benefited all residents in our coverage areas.

## A Collaborative Effort

The successful security operations during G20 were a testament to what can be achieved when private security, law enforcement, and communities work together. We thank all our partners for their collaboration.

## Lessons Learned

The experience of supporting G20 operations has strengthened our capabilities and reinforced the importance of public-private partnerships in community safety. We will continue to apply these lessons in our day-to-day operations.
