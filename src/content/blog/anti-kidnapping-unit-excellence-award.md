We are proud to announce that Vision Tactical's Anti-Kidnapping Unit (AKU) has been awarded the 2025 Provincial Commissioner's Excellence Award by the South African Police Service.

## Recognition for Outstanding Service

This prestigious award recognises the exceptional work our Anti-Kidnapping Unit has done in partnership with SAPS over the past year. The AKU has successfully resolved 94 kidnapping cases, rescuing victims and bringing perpetrators to justice.

## Pro-Bono Service to Communities

What makes this achievement even more remarkable is that our Anti-Kidnapping Unit operates entirely pro-bono. We do not charge victims' families for our services. We believe that no family should have to choose between their loved one's safety and their financial security.

## Partnership with SAPS

Our success is built on strong partnerships. We work closely with SAPS, sharing intelligence and coordinating operations to maximise our impact. This collaborative approach has proven highly effective in combating the kidnapping epidemic that has plagued our communities.

## A Team Effort

This award belongs to every member of our Anti-Kidnapping Unit, our control room staff, our drone pilots, and our armed response officers who support AKU operations. It also belongs to our community partners who trust us with their safety.

## Looking Ahead

As we accept this award, we renew our commitment to protecting Johannesburg communities. We will continue to innovate, to train, and to serve with the utmost professionalism.

Thank you to the Provincial Commissioner, to SAPS, and to the communities we serve.
